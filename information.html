Accommodations

For guests looking to be where the action is, we recommend staying in Waikiki, an area of metropolitan Honolulu. There is no shortage of things to do here, both indoors and outdoors.

For guests looking for some peace and quiet, we recommend staying on the North Shore of Oahu where the wedding will take place. Some of the best beaches are found on this side of the island. Hotel choices are limited here, so a vacation rental may be a better option.
